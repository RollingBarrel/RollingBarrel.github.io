Chrysalis

Release Date: November 08th, 2024

Genres: Top down shooter | Hack & Slash

Synopsis

In a distant future, the head of security at a research facility on a remote planet faces an unforeseen threat. The facility has been extracting a mysterious energy from the planet’s core, an ancient entity, aiming to replace depleting energy sources.

After a devastating quake, his wife, the head scientist, becomes trapped in the lower levels. As he descends to rescue her, the protagonist battles corrupted security robots and mutated test subjects that have escaped. Upon reaching the core, he discovers his wife has been corrupted by the entity, forcing him to make a final, crucial decision.

Characters

Elios

As the head of security for the facility, the protagonist is highly trained in combat, tactics, and security protocols. His mission is to rescue his wife, the head scientist, but as he ventures deeper into the facility, he is forced to confront not only external threats but also his personal connection to the mysterious energy at the core of the planet.

Chrysalis

Formerly the head of research, Chrysalis is now a fusion of her human form and the ancient energy entity. Her body has transformed, with pulsating energy and corrupted machinery woven into her being. She represents the final test of the protagonist's strength, wielding both power and sorrow in a terrifying amalgamation of science and the unknown.

Characteristics:
  • Corporate soldier
  • Hard-Wired Gear
  • Relentless and unstoppable

EdgeGuard

A humanoid security robot designed for close-quarters combat. The Edgeguard is equipped with a massive, energy blade integrated into one arm, which it uses to strike swiftly and decisively at any intruder. Its robust design and AI-driven combat system make it a formidable opponent in narrow corridors and enclosed spaces.

Characteristics:
  • Slashing Mech
  • Relentless and unstoppable
  • Corporate soldier

Neutralizer Vanguard

A ranged security robot similar in design to a bipedal walker, reminiscent of ancient war machines. The Neutralizer Vanguard is heavily armed, with long-range blasters capable of tracking and eliminating targets from afar. Its primary function is to suppress and neutralize threats before they can get too close to vital areas.

Characteristics:
  • Corporate soldier
  • Hard-Wired Gear
  • Relentless and unstoppable

BlastBall

The Blastball is a small, spherical enemy designed for a single purpose: to pursue and eliminate its target through self-detonation. Equipped with sophisticated tracking sensors, it locks onto its prey and relentlessly chases them down. Once within range, it primes itself and detonates in a massive explosion, causing catastrophic damage to anything in the vicinity

Characteristics:
  • Innovative Tech
  • Big Blast
  • 1 Objective: YOU

Bonepiercers

Once human test subjects, the Bonepiercers have mutated into grotesque creatures, their bones erupting from their bodies and forming deadly spear-like appendages. These floating entities have a single large eye in the center of their faces, which glows with an eerie orange light as they hunt down intruders.

Characteristics:
  • Quick & lethal
  • Fast Approaching
  • Short-Tempered

Gazeweepers

A variant of the Bonepiercer, the Gazeweeper has further mutated, with its flesh turning a sickly purple and its bone structure glowing a toxic green. Instead of relying on melee attacks, it uses its large central eye to fire concentrated laser beams, making it a deadly ranged foe.

Characteristics:
  • Dazzling Sniper Gaze
  • Filled with blind rage
  • Fast & Deadly
