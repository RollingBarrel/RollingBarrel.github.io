Synopsis

In a distant future, the head of security at a research facility on a remote planet faces an unforeseen threat. The facility has been extracting a mysterious energy from the planet’s core, an ancient entity, aiming to replace depleting energy sources.

After a devastating quake, his wife, the head scientist, becomes trapped in the lower levels. As he descends to rescue her, the protagonist battles corrupted security robots and mutated test subjects that have escaped. Upon reaching the core, he discovers his wife has been corrupted by the entity, forcing him to make a final, crucial decision.
