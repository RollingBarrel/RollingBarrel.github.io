About Us

We are a team of passionate game developers, currently pursuing our Master's degree in Art and Programming AAA Games at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC). Our team is composed of students from different parts of Spain and around the world, united by our love for creating immersive gaming experiences.

For the past eight months, we have been working on our first major project, Chrysalis a top-down, action-packed hack and slash game that combines shooting mechanics with unique abilities and a variety of ammunition types. Our goal is to deliver fast-paced, exciting gameplay that challenges and entertains players.

Chrysalis

Chrysalis is a top-down action-packed hack and slash set in a high-tech sci-fi laboratory, where an energy extraction experiment has gone catastrophically wrong. As the head of security, you must traverse through multiple levels of the facility to save your wife—the head scientist—who has become trapped deep within the complex after a violent disturbance.

  • High-octane hack and slash combat with top-down shooting mechanics.
  • Face a variety of enemies, from security robots to mutated monsters.
  • Uncover a gripping narrative set in a dystopian sci-fi world.
  • Battle against powerful bosses that will test your skills and strategy.
