About Us

We are a team of programmers, 3d artists, animators and game designers from all walks of life gathering to create fun and inventive hack and slash video games. Currently working on Paradox.

Paradox

Top down shooter and hack slash in a high tech laboratory where there has been an outbreak of paranormal creatures. Mixing high paced combat with an unsettling and distorted setting that will keep the player on alert and uncertain of what is going to happen.

  • Element System.
  • Strategic Combat.
  • Different fighting options.
