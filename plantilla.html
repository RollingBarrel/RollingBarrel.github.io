About Us

We are a team of passionate game developers, currently pursuing our Master's degree in Art and Programming AAA Games at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC). Our team is composed of students from different parts of Spain and around the world, united by our love for creating immersive gaming experiences.

For the past eight months, we have been working on our first major project, Chrysalis a top-down, action-packed hack and slash game that combines shooting mechanics with unique abilities and a variety of ammunition types. Our goal is to deliver fast-paced, exciting gameplay that challenges and entertains players.
